Arrows for Revival
ASF UNIZIK 017 set
Back

Whenever God intends to do anything unique and spectacular in any generation, He singles out person(s) whom He uses as a channel to make known His counsel to men. Currently, we are experiencing so many mind-blowing occurrences in our generation today. We experience a lot of moral decadence, corruptions, in different sectors in the land.

The devil and his cohort seem to have taken over every sphere of life, ranging from the government to education, entertainment, media, health, finance etc and worst of it all , they have crept into the body of Christ and are currently wrecking havocs. The truth is that darkness has filled the earth and thick darkness has covered the people. Children use their parents for ritual, young persons who were counseled to remember their creator in the days of their youth now indulge in prostitution, armed robbery, drug trafficking etc. The political office holders now siphon public funds into personal accounts, whereas the poor masses are left to suffer and sort themselves out, the media and entertainment sectors now take their time to produce works that end up mocking our God and faith. Most times I ask myself, "Is it a taboo for believers to rise up and occupy these positions, correct the wrongs therein and do exploit for God?"

Matthew 5:14-16 made us to understand that the world is in darkness, and that the believers are the only ones who possess the light to lighten up the world (paraphrased). However, I wonder why we now scare away from showing the light for the world to see and become better.

A lot of thoughts began flowing into our minds on the plans of God concerning our lives and tomorrow when we leave campus. As we waited on God in the place of prayer to know His commissioning words to us, he first told us that a besieging army is coming from a distant land against our land (Jeremiah 4:16). This is true; the devil has besieged almost everywhere and many believers are only finding a place to stand. Even the church is being besieged too, currently, there are intra and inter-denominational crises in the body of Christ; nobody seem to agree with the other.

Secondly, God made it clear to us from Psalms 127:3-5, that He needs sons whom He will use as arrows (weapons of warfare) in order to contend with the enemies at the gate and stir up revival in the land. Hence the name: "ARROWS FOR REVIVAL"

Initially, these words were heavy, but as sought for more clarity in the place of prayers and bible study, we received clear bearing and specific instructions among which are;
  • Arrows are made and shaped for effective and specific assignments, therefore, we must submit ourselves to be made.
  • The arrowhead (Christ) and stabilizing fins/feather (Holy Spirit) are very vital as we journey in the master’s plan. An arrow without a head is just a mere stick.
  • The extent of harm (effectiveness) that an arrow can cause depends on the sharpness of the arrowhead. (Christ in me).
  • The shaft (ourselves) must be dried up and made weightless, indicating that we must get rid of every weight, encumbrances, sins etc that shall make us heavy. The only weight of an arrow is that imposed on it by the arrowhead and fins.

As Arrows for Revival, it may not be clear to us now where our master (the warlord) intends to shoot us to, but we are rest assured that we shall represent Him well without missing our target. We shall not be missing in His quiver. To those He intends to shoot into the political, educational, agricultural, media, entertainment, mission field and even in our different homes, we ask for grace to submit ourselves for making, be found in His quiver always, be available for use whenever a need arises and go willingly in whatever direction, distance and assignment He desires.

Actually, it is one thing to catch this vision, and another thing altogether to run with it. We therefore solicit your unrelenting labour in the place of prayer for to God succeed with our lives.

Your Brother,
Ogboso Reuben Arua

PEOPLE NEED YOUR PRODUCT

The disciple remained in Jerusalem when Jesus ascended. Even after receiving the promised Holy Spirit. But God used Saul the persecutor to actualize the charge He gave the disciples while on earth in Acts 1:8. "But Saul began to destroy the Church. Going from house to house, he dragged off men and women and put them in prison. Those who had been scattered preached the word wherever they went." Acts 8:3-4 .

The plan of God is that after the Holy Spirit has fallen on the disciples that they will preach the Good news first in Jerusalem, to Judea and Samaria and to the ends of the earth. However, when they received the Holy Spirit they remained in Jerusalem Preaching, healing the sick, fellowshipping together, breaking bread and sharing things in common. But people out there were needing their PRODUCT!

Hence, Saul became an instrument of persecution and he scattered the church in Jerusalem. Lets see Philip in Samaria (Acts 8:4-8), The people of Samaria really needed somebody to show them goodness (Ps. 4:6). Permit me to say that to see light is their utmost desire. For when Simon the sorcerer proffered the counterfeit light they all gave in to his teaching; "…and all the people both high and low, gave him their attention and exclaimed, this man is the divine power known as the great power."

"My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge …" these are the words of God in Hosea 4:6. The people of Samaria being in darkness yearned for light. Satan on his part used that as an avenue to provide a counterfeit light, so as to destroy as many lives as he could, he still works with the principle of lies till today. He used the sorcerer to subject the Samaritans to abject darkness. All these while light has been hiding itself under a bushel somewhere in Jerusalem.

Why subject yourself under while you have the product which people out there need? Do you know that whatsoever the Lord did, satan tries to imitate. Satan is drawing many souls who long for you product to hell on daily basis simply because you chose to keep your light under bushel. Jesus teaching the beatitudes said, "You are the light of the world, a city on a hill cannot be hidden, neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bushel. Instead they put it on its stand, and it gives light to everyone in the house" matt. 5:14-15.

Let your light shine. Rise up and Shine! People are wandering in the storm because they need this light. Satan on his part is constantly pushing men deeper into damnation. The lives of the Samaritans changed tremendously on the arrival of the original light. Even Simon the Sorcerer desired this original light; "Philip went down to a city in Samaria and proclaimed the Christ there. When the crowds heard Philip and saw the miraculous signs he did. With shrieks, evil spirits came out of many and many paralytics and cripples were healed. So there was great joy in the city!" Acts 8:5-8. It is note-worthy that the appearance of the light, made Satan to go into oblivion. There was a great joy in the city, "Yes! This is the actual thing we need", they deserted the sorcerer and followed the Light. The sorcerer himself also desired for the undiluted Light.

Afterwards, the angel of the Lord said to Philip, "Go south to the road, the desert road that goes down from Jerusalem to Gaza, Acts 8:26".

I will like to state; If you really wish to impact lives with your product there is no idle waiting. Even Jesus had to go places where His Product was needed, John 4:3-4. The people of Galilee needed the Product of Jesus. On His way to supply the products to the Galileans, He had to go through Samaria because the Samaritan woman would be waiting by the well of Samaria to receive her portion. Her desire is the fountain that gives eternal life and Jesus had it. Get up, locate people that need your product, while you are looking for them, believe that you will find them.

The holy spirit of God said to Philip "Go South to the desert road…" , there is an Ethiopian eunuch reading the bible along that road but he is not understanding, Philip is with the product which the Ethiopian desired. He explained to this eunuch what his soul needed. He told him about Jesus. As they travelled along the road, they came across some water and Philip baptized him. When they stepped out of the water the Holy Spirit took him away to other people that were in need of his product. The eunuch went on his way rejoicing.

"Philip however appeared in Azotus and travelled about, preaching the gospel in all the towns until he reached Ceasarea."

In every life God has deposited a product which other person(s) dearly desires. You will begin to succeed with your life when the hurt and problems of others begins to matter to you. Jesus hurts when others hurt. Heb. 4:15. Those who unlock your compassion are those to whom your life have been deposited with the product which they desire. Jesus has the product which the woman of Nain. He had to go to the city of Nain at the time the woman needed Him most. Her son is been carried out to be buried (Luke 7:11-17).

Brethren, why not arose now there is still day. Jesus said "I will do the work of He who sent me now there is day, for night is coming from when no man can work" John 9:4. At the appearance of the light darkness must give way. The people of Samaria were held in darkness by Simon the sorcerer until Philip (Light) surfaced. Hence, Christians ought to make sure that the light is kept shining so that darkness will not hold the church bound.

The church must march on! The gates of hell will never prevail over it. Philip hide not your Identity, Get up! Seek those that are in need of your product.

Then said Jesus to the twelve, Will you also go away?" Many have gone away, great men and women, people that we have looked up to and desired to be like. But why do they go? Men like Daniel , Hanniah, Azariah and Mishael came to a complete resolution in their hearts, and deliberately went against the instructions of King Nebuchadnezzar. Only when the matter is settled in your heart will you then be able to stand out.

Can you hear the conclusion, even though it is hard, decide to remain in Him. Where else can eternity be secured if not in Christ? What is worth exchanging for an ever-lasting life in Christ? Do you know tomorrow? It is clear that Daniel and the three Hebrew youths had no alternative except Jesus Christ.

Prayerfully make a commitment with this song:

I have made up my mind to serve you for life
Breathe on me, O Lord breathe on me
This is my decision for life there is no going back
Breathe on me, O Lord breathe.

Bro Chidera (Graduate)

This is a digital year book for the 2016/2017 session's Final years of Anglican Students fellowship, Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka (Unizik).

This application gives you access to the profiles of the fellowship's final year students, provides a gallery containing pictures of Final years, alumni and some present students, it equally contains some goodwill messages and articles.

This idea was conceived and implemented by Okwuosa Chijioke a member of the above mentioned fellowship.

Okwuosa Chijioke is a website developer, a mobile application developer and currently into Digital marketing, he is a graduate of Computer science, Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, Anambra State, Nigeria.
